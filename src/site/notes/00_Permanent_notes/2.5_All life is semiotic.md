# All life is semiotic.

Everything that surrounds us is a sign that contains information; the study of signs is semiotics. For this reason, we can say that the study of life is overlapping with the study of semiotics.


>[[00_VOULT/02_REFERENCE NOTES/How Forests Think_Eduardo Kohn_2021\|How Forests Think_Eduardo Kohn_2021]]
> "All beings, including non-human ones, are constitutively semiotic. All life is semiotic and all semiosis is alive. In decisive ways, then, life and thought are the exact same thing: life thinks; thoughts are alive."
