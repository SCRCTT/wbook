# Negotiation is key — Eleonora Fabião.

Think of negotiation as a weapon for the present; we live in a time of bureaucracies and institutions. For this reason, Eleonora Fabião has proposed unconventional institutions born from negotiation with real institutions—policies that are part of the community.

Institutions are, above all, made of people, and structures have their own rules. By playing with these rules, it is possible to expose the strengths and weaknesses of a structure.

[[00_Permanent_notes/1.3a1_We here between sky and earth_2021_Eleonora Fabião\|1.3a1_We here between sky and earth_2021_Eleonora Fabião]]

The questions she might propose are:

- **Which action generates the movement that makes the city the city I want to live in?**
- **What are the structures that hinder me?** (We often assume there are structures that act as obstacles, but perhaps they are merely structures that can be navigated or overcome through negotiation).
