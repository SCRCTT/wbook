Only the stranger, the one who is not part of a community, can actually show its strangeness, or the hidden part. The extraneous actor manages to make the place he sees reconsider.


> [[00_VOULT/02_REFERENCE NOTES/atti_della_disputa_sulla-natura_del_teatro_Giuseppe Bartolucci, Romeo Castellucci, Rubina Giorgi, Raimondo Guarino, Renata Molinari e Mario Perniola_Edizioni Casa del Bello Estremo, Cesena, 1990\|atti_della_disputa_sulla-natura_del_teatro_Giuseppe Bartolucci, Romeo Castellucci, Rubina Giorgi, Raimondo Guarino, Renata Molinari e Mario Perniola_Edizioni Casa del Bello Estremo, Cesena, 1990]]
> For a reconquest of vision, Romeo Castellucci

**> The actor is the inexperienced, the stranger**:
> it is the actor who is the messenger, the one who always comes from a very distant place, who makes us reconsider the place where he happened. He is the one who reveals the local a-priori, The mythical a-priori. Only the stranger can reveal the strangeness of the place.
