# alter-politics or the study of other ways of being

Just as when one studies and compares the ant system. The study of alter-politics helps us critique our own way of being through other ways of being.


> [[00_VOULT/02_REFERENCE NOTES/How Forests Think_Eduardo Kohn_2021\|How Forests Think_Eduardo Kohn_2021]]
>"Ghassan Hage (2012) defines 'alter-politics'—a politics that does not develop from opposition to our current systems or from their critique, but develops from the analysis of another way of being, which here involves other living beings."
