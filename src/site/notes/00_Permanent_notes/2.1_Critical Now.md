# Critical Now

What is critical today? Where must we place our attention, what are the urgencies?

There are performative acts like the red drones over Copenhagen airport. A performance of violence and threat. What kind of expression should be used? To achieve what? What tools, documentary theater? Facts and fiction?

We must return to concreteness to awaken the dormant contemporary performance scene.
