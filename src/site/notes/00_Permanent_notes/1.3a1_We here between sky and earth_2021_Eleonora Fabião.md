# 0.1_We, here, between sky and earth, 2021, Eleonora Fabião

He negotiated to obtain a chair from each of Brazil's 27 states. The difficulty of negotiating is obtaining a chair from the institutions. On November 27th, he invited all the representatives to a networking meeting with 27 chairs, just like the state chairs.
He returned the chairs, but by exchanging the chairs for institutions, they will get their own chairs back, but from a different institution (so the hospital chair goes to the theater, etc.). By doing so, they will not only get a chair back, but an artistic project, creating art galleries in hospitals, theaters, and other places.
