# Non-human studying the human through the other

Studying the human through non-humans—that is, studying the signs and representations of animals (etc.) to study the human.

It is a very significant shift in thought.

This facilitates the understanding of a decentralized world made of forces that we forget exist. Behaviors and communities that we do not know because we have never identified them.
