# For a reconquest of vision, Romeo Castellucci

## The actor is the inexperienced, the stranger

It is the actor who is the messenger, the one who always comes from a very distant place, who makes us reconsider the place where he happened. He is the one who reveals the local a-priori, The mythical a-priori. Only the stranger can reveal the strangeness of the place.
